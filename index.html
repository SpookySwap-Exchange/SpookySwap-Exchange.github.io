Frequently Asked Questions

How is SpookySwap different from a multi-chain aggregator?

SpookySwap focuses on native Fantom liquidity and optimized AMM routing within Fantom. This keeps gas low and settlement fast. Aggregators span many DEXs/chains; Spooky aims to be best-in-class on Fantom.

Which assets and pools are best for low slippage?

Stable pools are ideal for correlated assets (e.g., USDC/USDT), while volatile pools serve non-correlated pairs. Check pool depth and recent volume; deeper liquidity = better price stability.

How can I reduce MEV risk on Fantom?
  • Use tight but realistic slippage and avoid peak volatility.
  • Consider private/builder RPCs if your wallet supports them.
  • Split larger trades into smaller transactions.
What should LPs watch out for?

Impermanent loss on volatile pairs; protocol fee tiers; and utilization. For correlated assets, stable pools reduce IL. Monitor APR/fees vs. IL to assess net returns.

Why can realized output differ from the initial quote?

Pool prices shift as trades land; gas and inclusion time matter. Always evaluate effective price after fees and slippage, not just the headline quote.
